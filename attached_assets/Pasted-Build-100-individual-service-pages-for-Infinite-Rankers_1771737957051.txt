SECTION 4: ANIMATIONS (EVERY PAGE)
════════════════════════════════════════

Page Load:
- Preloader: logo appears, fades out (0.8s)
- Hero text: staggered char-by-char reveal
- Hero visual: slides in from right with fade
- Stats counter: triggers on scroll

Scroll Animations (IntersectionObserver):
- Every section fades up: opacity 0→1, translateY 30px→0
- Stagger delay on grid children: 0.1s, 0.2s, 0.3s...
- Process line draws from left to right on scroll
- Stat counters count from 0 to final number (2s ease)
- Benefit cards pop in with slight scale: 0.95→1

Hover Interactions:
- Cards: translateY(-8px) + box-shadow glow
- Buttons: shimmer sweep + slight scale(1.03)
- Nav links: underline slides in from left
- Images: subtle zoom (scale 1→1.05)
- Related service cards: border gradient reveal

Custom Cursor (site-wide):
- Outer ring: 32px, border 1.5px solid #3A5FBF
- Inner dot: 5px solid #8B5CF6
- rAF lerp: 0.13 (fast, smooth, no lag)
- On hover links: ring scales to 52px, fills blue 10% opacity
